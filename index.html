Methodology & Caveats

›
  • What's included: product releases, press, M&A and positioning from publicly available sources. Sources are clickable inside each card.
  • Snapshot: data is from this run, not live. The report is regenerated each time the competitor list is run through.
  • New BC-native players: Lasernet, AMC Banking, Acubiz and Microsoft's own Expense Agent, plus Pagero, B2Brouter and Qvalia.
  • Category note: Dime Scheduler is BC-native but included as an ISV neighbour, not a direct AP rival.
  • LinkedIn engagement is tracked on the Content page — public reactions, comments and reposts across each competitor's last 10 posts (public counts only, not impressions).
  • YouTube figures per video for the last month cannot be reliably fetched automatically.
